What's in the Water?

Settlers on the Oregon Trail used to stop in Soda Springs for water from one of the nearly thousand fresh mineral water springs. The naturally carbonated water is still an attraction for this small Idaho city. Hooper Springs has a pavilion over a carbonated spring where to this day you can still dip a cup into the spring to try some of the water.
